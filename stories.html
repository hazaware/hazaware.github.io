Sitio Bebe

A small town in Pampanga called Sitio Bebe is rich in natural resources and so most of the people here works in mining or in factories. Working in that kind of environment makes them prone to sickness especially acute respiratory infections.

That is where knowledge about safety measures in your workplace is important. It prevents the workers from having diseases. Wearing proper preventive tools and following safety measures allows them to be in a safe workplace.
